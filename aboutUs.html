Trishula Maritime Private Limited

This organization is run by a family of seafarers backed by 2 generation of service to marine fraternity enriched with multiple decades of working, teaching and management experience in maritime and offshore sector. Our team is complemented by Senior Master Mariners, Senior Ch Engineers, Class/MWS surveyors and Senior Manning Experts. This expertise enables us to provide with various kind of expert solutions to our esteemed clients.

With our vast experience in the feld we will be able to provide various kind of expert solutions to ship owners and ensures for safe and economical operation of their vessels, complying with international safety and environmental protection norms. We are successfully running a Maritime Training Institute approved by DG Shipping and IMO for last 7 years. https://tridentmaritime.in

Our Institute is Renowned DG Shipping (Directorate General of Shipping-Ministry of Shipping) approved for conducting basic safety courses for seafarers. We are offering courses with motto to impart quality training to seafarers to get compliance with requirements of STCW-1978(As Amended)/ISPS Code/ISM Code, SOLAS, MARPOL, IMCA, Oil Companies (Charterer's specific training ) , Safety and security training.

The Institute is audited and Certified for ISO 9001:2015 Quality standards by Renowned ABS Quality Standards(American Bureau of Shipping). we have to our credit many happy and satisfied ship owners as clients.

A Versatile Marine Service provider company for today's Shipping needs. We believe in being accountable for our clients, our employees, our owners and the environment.
