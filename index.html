OUR SERVICES

We take pride in offering a wide range of services to the ever-growing needs of the marine industry and constantly push the boundaries for Commerce, Competence, Excellence, and Cooperation.

CREW & TECHNICAL MANAGEMENT

We supply hardworking and well-qualified crew to Ro-Ro, bulk carriers, containers, oil tankers, chemical tankers, and gas carriers. We provide end-to-end solutions.

TRADING & DEMO VESSELS

We specialize in providing capable crew for reactivating laid-up vessels or taking over vessels for delivery voyages to ship breakers' yards.

SHIP REPAIRS & SPARE SUPPLIES

We monitor the spare parts market constantly to enable us to deliver quality aftermarket spares for main engines, generator engines, and auxiliary equipment.

MARINE CONSULTANCY SERVICES

We offer experienced and skilled professionals who can handle a variety of shipping requirements and offer cost-competitive, comprehensive advisory services.

GREEN RECYCLING

We have taken proactive measures to be responsible in ship recycling to adopt methods that protect the environment and comply with international standards.
